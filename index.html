Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need boating experience to rent a yacht?

No, all of our yacht rentals come with a professional captain and crew. You don't need any boating experience to enjoy a luxury yacht charter with us.

What's included in the rental price?

Our rental prices include the yacht, professional captain, crew, fuel for standard cruising, water toys (varies by yacht), and standard amenities. Food, beverages, and special requests are typically additional.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for regular days and 4-6 weeks for holidays and peak season dates. However, we can sometimes accommodate last-minute bookings depending on availability.

What happens in case of bad weather?

If severe weather conditions prevent safe boating, we'll work with you to reschedule your charter at no additional cost. Our captains make the final safety decisions regarding weather conditions.

Can I bring my own food and drinks?

Yes, you're welcome to bring your own food and beverages. We can also arrange catering services or recommend local options if you prefer. Some of our yachts have cooking facilities and refrigeration.
